## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: Midnight Glass Visuals
All map bottom sheets MUST use a dark translucent background to align with the premium branding.

#### Scenario: Exploration sheet style
- **WHEN** the map is in exploration mode
- **THEN** the bottom sheet SHALL have a dark blur background
- **AND** the handle indicator SHALL be light gray (`rgba(255,255,255,0.2)`).

### Requirement: Apple-Style Search Input
The search bar MUST be integrated into the bottom sheet as a floating, minimal element.

#### Scenario: Search bar appearance
- **WHEN** the search bar is displayed
- **THEN** it SHALL have a semi-transparent dark background
- **AND** the placeholder text SHALL be white with 40% opacity
- **AND** the search icon SHALL be Solar Gold.

### Requirement: High-Contrast POI Content
All text and icons within the sheets MUST be optimized for readability against a dark translucent background.

#### Scenario: POI Detail visibility
- **WHEN** a POI is selected
- **THEN** the title SHALL be white
- **AND** secondary labels SHALL be silver/light gray.
