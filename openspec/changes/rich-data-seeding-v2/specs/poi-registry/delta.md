## Modified Requirements

### Requirement: Spatial Density and POI Diversity

The POI registry MUST support high spatial density (multiple POIs per site) and diverse categorization to test map filtering and clustering.

#### Scenario: Site Clustering

- **WHEN** the system is seeded with "Rich Seed v2"
- **THEN** major event sites MUST be surrounded by a cluster of 4-5 POIs.
- **AND** the cluster MUST include at least 3 different POI types (e.g., a restaurant, a parking spot, and a lounge).
